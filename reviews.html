Overview

It looks and weighs like a dinosaur, its a two tonne SUV, which consumes fuel at a alarming rate. However it performs not just on the road but is made for off-roading as well. It all comes down to the powerful handdbuilt 4.0L V8 engine that powers the car. It packs a punch of 503HP, which brings the car from 0-100 km/hr in 3.8seconds frightening figures for a car that weighs 2000kg. A 9-speed gearbox with lightning shifts come standard with the car with the rear-biased 4Matic all wheel drive system.

Drive

The V8 rumble can be heard significantly in the cabin bringing smiles to the driver as well as the passengers alike with pops and crackles coming out from the exhaust as u downshift through the gears as well as the distinguish DSG farts when u upshift while in sports mode. The throttle response from the engine is instant as well, having close to zero turbo lag.

The weight of the vehicle starts to show as you start attacking the corners, being a SUV it has a high center of gravity and u start to feel the rear wheels slipping when u drive on the limit. However the greatest complain that I have while driving the car is that even on comfort mode the suspension still feels too stiff and bumpy, and it is not something that I expected from a mercedes SUV.

Overview

The 2023 Audi RS3 represents the pinnacle of Audi's performance-oriented compact sedan lineup. This sporty and dynamic vehicle continues the tradition of blending everyday practicality with exhilarating performance. The 2023 RS3 showcases Audi's distinctive design language with a bold and aggressive front grille, RS-specific styling elements, and a sleek and aerodynamic profile. It exudes a sense of athleticism and sophistication, making a striking impression on the road.

Drive

Under the hood, the 2023 Audi RS3 packs a punch with a turbocharged 2.5-liter inline-five-cylinder engine. This engine is capable of producing impressive horsepower and torque figures, allowing the RS3 to accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in just a matter of seconds. Audi's quattro all-wheel-drive system ensures exceptional traction and stability, making it suitable for various driving conditions.

The RS3's performance isn't limited to straight-line speed; its precision-tuned suspension and sharp steering deliver an engaging and confidence-inspiring driving experience. Whether you're tackling winding mountain roads or navigating city streets, the RS3 offers a sporty and responsive ride.

Overview

It is still powered by the twin-straight six engine under its hood, pushing out 502 HP and 650 Nm, with it being rear-wheel drive and still being a 4 door saloon. That much is carried over by its predecessor. However, the previous 7-speed gearbox is being replaced by a 8-speed automatic gearbox. Even though it is part of the 3 series family, it is easy to identify a M3 due to its fierce looking bodykit that comes standard with it.

Drive

When we first test drove the car we were amazed by how quick and accurate the steering response is, giving the driver full confidence when attacking a corner at full speed as you will be able to know exactly how much the car will turn. It hands down has the best steering systems avaliable on any car in recent times.

When it comes to the power, we have always known that BMW loves to undermine the true performance that their car has. When we tested BMW cars on a dyno, the numbers that comes out are always 5-10% more than what BMW claims. The power felt on this car is definately more than 650Nm the push that the engine gave was amazing. Throttle response was epic as well it feels like a drive by wire throttle.
